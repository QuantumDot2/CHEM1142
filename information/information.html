Final Exam Information

The final exam in Chem 1142 is an ACS standardized exam, covering material from chapters 1 through 21 of our textbook. The SSU chemistry club sells a study guide for this exam. I’ve prepared some notes on the exam and the grading curve. The ACS has some useful tips on taking their standardized exams too.

Some exams with a similar format to the ACS final are located on this webpage. (ACS High School Olympiad.)

Also, the New York Regents high-school exams are at a similar difficulty to the ACS final.

If you'd like to practice material mainly from CHEM1141, and a little from CHEM1142... but at a high-school level... the state of Virginia has a nice little multiple choice exam generator that you can try.
